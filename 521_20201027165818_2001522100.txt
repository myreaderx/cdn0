!important;word-wrap: break-word !important;\">餐饮业也有风险，主要表现在</span><strong><span style=\"max-width: 100%;font-size: 16px;color: rgb(214, 168, 65);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: 100%;min-height: 1em;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, \" helvetica neue sc sans gb yahei ui arial sans-serif normal border-box break-word><span style=\"max-width: 100%;font-size: 16px;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;\"><br></span></p><p style=\"max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, \" helvetica style=\"max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, \" helvetica neue sc sans gb yahei ui arial sans-serif normal border-box break-word><span BlinkMacSystemFont, \" helvetica neue sc sans gb yahei ui arial sans-serif normal border-box break-word><span style=\"max-width: 100%;font-size: 16px;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: border-box break-word><span style=\"max-width: 100%;font-size: 16px;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;\">《第一财经》杂志曾在今年6月刊</span><a
