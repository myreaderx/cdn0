Hand-painted details adorn the sculpture&#8217;s exterior, along with found objects like antique prosthetic eyes, deer antlers, and ball feet.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<div id=\"attachment_138554\" style=\"width: 2010px\" class=\"wp-caption alignnone\"><img aria-describedby=\"caption-attachment-138554\" loading=\"lazy\" class=\"wp-image-138554 size-full\" src=\"https://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/bothwell-4.jpg\" alt=\"\" width=\"2000\" height=\"1436\" srcset=\"https://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/bothwell-4.jpg 2000w, https://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/bothwell-4-640x460.jpg 640w, https://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/bothwell-4-960x689.jpg 960w, https://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/bothwell-4-1536x1103.jpg 1536w, https://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/bothwell-4-624x448.jpg 624w, https://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/bothwell-4-640x460@2x.jpg 1280w, https://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/bothwell-4-960x689@2x.jpg 1920w\" sizes=\"(max-width: 2000px) 100vw, 2000px\" /><p id=\"caption-attachment-138554\" class=\"wp-caption-text\">&#8220;Soul Sentinel&#8221; (2017), cast glass, ceramic, oil paint, and antique wood doll puppet hands, 21 inches</p></div>\n<p>The result of this months-long technique is a surreal collection that merges the organic forms and processes of nature with uncanny details. Each lusterless piece explores the relationship between the alluring oddities of the exterior and the translucent insides, which Bothwell explains:</p>\n<blockquote><p>Changing the body is merely adjusting the outer wrapping, as far as I can see&#8230; I am intrigued with the spirit world, and I imagine that we pass in and out of it, into the physical realm with bodies, then out of it at the end of life into lighter, energy bodies&#8230; And along the way throughout our lives, we transform ourselves constantly, reinventing who we are on a daily basis.</p></blockquote>\n<p>Bothwell will be featured in an upcoming episode of PBS&#8217;s <a href=\"https://www.pbs.org/show/craft-in-america/\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\"><em>Craft in America</em></a> airing on December 11.
