data-rte-list=\"default\"><li><p class=\"\">Click the “<strong>Draw</strong>” tab<em> (see image below; if you don’t see it, your version of PowerPoint doesn’t have it, see below). </em></p></li><li><p class=\"\">Click on the pen or highlighter you want to use<em>.</em></p></li><li><p class=\"\">Click the down arrow to choose the size<em> (and/or to change the color). </em></p></li><li><p class=\"\">Draw/write what you want. </p></li><li><p class=\"\">Select the pieces you want to animate<em> (usually you’ll select everything you just drew). </em></p></li><li><p class=\"\">Click the “<strong>Animation</strong>” tab. </p></li><li><p class=\"\">Click <strong>“Ink Replay.” </strong><em>(see image below; if it’s not clickable, it’s because you haven’t selected the pieces you just drew).</em> If you don’t even see it, your version of PowerPoint doesn’t have it (see below). </p></li><li><p class=\"\">Adjust the animation settings (e.g, Duration) so it goes the speed you want, and only animates when you want it to.   </p></li></ol>&#160;\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n  \n\n    \n  \n    \n\n      \n\n      \n        <figure class=\"\n
