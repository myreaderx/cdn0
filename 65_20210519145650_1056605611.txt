Use decorators caulk to hide all the joints. </p>\n\n\n\n<p>7. Put the doors back on.</p>\n\n\n\n<figure class=\"wp-block-image size-large\"><img loading=\"lazy\" width=\"766\" height=\"674\" src=\"https://www.ikeahackers.net/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/havsta-built-in-10a.jpg\" alt=\"IKEA HAVSTA storage combination built-in hack\" class=\"wp-image-83045\" srcset=\"https://www.ikeahackers.net/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/havsta-built-in-10a.jpg 766w, https://www.ikeahackers.net/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/havsta-built-in-10a-550x484.jpg 550w\" sizes=\"(max-width: 766px) 100vw, 766px\" /></figure>\n\n\n\n<p>12.
