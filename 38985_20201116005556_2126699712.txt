The table lets you pair up with up to 6 different players at the same time, playing games and bonding with each other while you&#8217;re socially distancing from your individual homes. If you&#8217;re playing with the family, the Infinity Game Table offers hours of entertainment and fun, allowing you to sit at home and play over 50 popular and indie games, thanks to partnerships with gaming giants like Hasbro among others. Speakers underneath the table, along with tactile vibration motors make the gaming experience multi-sensorial, allowing you to immerse yourself while playing.
