更符合未来趋势，相比之下磁吸接口稍显弱势。这是个取舍问题。</p><div class=\"image-desc\">\n          <img class=\"uploaded-img\" src=\"https://imgslim.geekpark.net/uploads/image/file/4f/dc/4fdc32b1c2abc9d2309dd98c45155716.png\" 磁吸配件中也包含对应磁体，可以轻松吸附，并且「叠加」使用。</p><div class=\"image-desc\">\n          <img class=\"uploaded-img\" src=\"https://imgslim.geekpark.net/uploads/image/file/81/4c/814c90b66fb63492effe9215c3c6d060.png\" 手机壳，包括一个可吸附在手机壳上的皮套。</p><div class=\"image-desc\">\n          <img class=\"uploaded-img\" src=\"https://imgslim.geekpark.net/uploads/image/file/2b/59/2b59e60fe3dafb09957d6506a8012d0a.gif\"
