I assume Control Key on PC</p>\n\n<p><img src=\"https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/sAmmaV3CrV-G9EtADRBLbdXaR9xr3QwZVDL8ZvSMrUPftCfeo00NDXAlA_uvhi1HLNevF5sF00XyUUQ4iQFEccoSvcDe31OX1EaB1B6pgl-IuP67WcZIeTH8xjzk55jp2s0o70_3\" /></p>\n\n<h3>Step 5</h3>\n\n<p>With the Rectangular Marquee Tool M select from the top until half of the ellipse holding the SHIFT tool. That means that you will add a selection.</p>\n\n<p><img src=\"https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/MLTsCASu2EL83qsFDbduhFKQP1EuKpPrs6CGJUp62xrloGtiwsDkuhfWuKUBXThvRQySOTy254KG5Z-R2ny9P9B9GV6HTPpfSrVxqMzuMUcULhxWpafvYVRpz3nbRE9xWQhmBMTs\" /></p>\n\n<h3>Step 6</h3>\n\n<p>Now mask the big circle.</p>\n\n<p><img src=\"https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/IaM0SfupigFfvo7bxHIgp5Cdp-_KddKxd3oZsuV0_PzLGKBpjWBD2bylGYyaVc0w15MO1ygQLJPQvp6Cuf4BiTtLELe_Q0D23I5e2JSTLDd8fPGkVcsiP-BO_dQYjXt-GeoZBs51\" /></p>\n\n<h3>Step 7</h3>\n\n<p>Now go to Filter>Blur Gallery>Tilt-Shift and then tweak the values depending on the size of your circle.
