No need for HTTP, you're just calling a clean method on <a href=\"http://feeds.hanselman.com/~/t/0/0/scotthanselman/~https://www.hanselman.com/blog/a-net-6-minimal-api-todo-example-playground\">the existing API</a>, directly.</p>\n<p>That's a simple example, just getting Todos.
