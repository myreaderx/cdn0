Judged by academics, prominent members of the press and successful professionals around the world, awarded designers are the minds behind frequently very compelling projects. Further, there are many categories (100 of them, in fact) covering just about every facet of modern design. Registration for the award is now open. You can find out more and register <a href=\"https://competition.adesignaward.com/registration.php\">here</a>.</p>\n<p>In the meantime, however, we take a look at past winners of one category: Arts, Crafts and Ready-Made design. This category encompasses works of art, crafted designs and ready-made objects as well as installations and functional sculptures. The Arts, Crafts and Ready-Made Design category would be of particular interest to professional and young artists, designers, design galleries, design and arts departments, and other art, craft and design oriented institutions in the creative industry around the world and all are encouraged to submit.</p>\n<p>In addition to a trophy, invitation to a gala celebration in Italy and numerous other perks of a lengthy prize package list, award winners are also offered the opportunity to exhibit their work, a free service, and make their work available for purchase free of any commission.
