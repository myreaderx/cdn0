border-box;\"><br style=\"box-sizing: border-box;\"><span style=\"color: rgb(51, 51, 51);\"><strong>放低姿态，才能真正得到别人的尊重与欣赏。</strong></span></p><p style=\"white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box;\"><br border-box;text-align: center;\"><strong><span style=\"color: rgb(255, 255, 255);background-color: rgb(110, 140, 192);\">- 04 -</span></strong></p><p style=\"white-space: normal;box-sizing: style=\"box-sizing: border-box;\"></p><p style=\"white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box;\"><br style=\"box-sizing: border-box;\"></p><p style=\"white-space: normal;box-sizing: style=\"box-sizing: border-box;\"></p><p style=\"white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box;\"><br style=\"box-sizing: border-box;\"></p><p style=\"white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box;text-align: center;\"><span style=\"color: rgb(255, 255, 255);background-color: rgb(110, 140, 192);\"><strong>- 07 - </strong></span></p><p style=\"white-space: normal;box-sizing:
