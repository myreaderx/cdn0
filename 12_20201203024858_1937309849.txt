Scientific discovery is not over but the big discoveries now are going to be found in places that are disputed or hostile,&#8221; noting that Colombia has been ravaged by a civil war for decades.</p>\n<p>Because of the breadth of the paintings—some are so high on the cliff that they only can be studied with drones—researchers believe the findings will take generations to study.
