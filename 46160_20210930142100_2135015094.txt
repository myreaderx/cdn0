The IC also offers write protection, which inhibits write operations by pulling the WP pin High (protects the entire memory). The external address pins make it possible to connect up to eight CAT24C512 EEPROM chips on the same I<sup>2</sup>C bus.</p> \n<p>\n <div class=\"center-block text-center\"> \n  <a href=\"https://cdn.sparkfun.com/assets/learn_tutorials/1/6/6/1/Qwiic_EEPROM_memory.jpg\"><img src=\"https://cdn.sparkfun.com/r/300-300/assets/learn_tutorials/1/6/6/1/Qwiic_EEPROM_memory.jpg\" alt=\"EEPROM IC\" /></a> \n </div> \n <div class=\"center-block text-center\"> \n  <em>EEPROM chip.
