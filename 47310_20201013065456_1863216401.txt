href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/039654dfb2491aa089bba26fd8917da5/#main\" rel=\"attachment wp-att-85354\"><img 个小时，然后回家疗伤，过两三个月再来开战。</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/0c77d6cb7d4ce93851338112022389b9/#main\" rel=\"attachment wp-att-85355\"><img 就算你有条大花臂，也不要以为自己什么都懂</p>\n<p>纹身是个非常高深的世界，就算是纹身师也都有不一样的艺术境界。</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/1d7f38121e4d84fd2090ad7d5297a415/#main\" href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/bce84ad181d3535c65b318272b978204/#main\" rel=\"attachment wp-att-85358\"><img 个亿里就算有 100 万个人想纹身，一个纹身师一天最多也就能处理2~3人，掐指一算，需要 1000 年才能完事… 忙哭了好吗！</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/14276d7ac183eb3748517838e07a145d/#main\" href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/640-69/#main\" rel=\"attachment wp-att-85359\"><img class=\"rank\" style=\" \">10.</strong> 去之前先饱餐一顿</p>\n<p>纹身前一天要确保有充足的睡眠，保持良好的皮肤状态，然后去店里之前先饱餐一顿。</p>\n<p>因为不知道你要搞多久，中间会不会低血糖，也不知道结束后你会不会太兴奋或太痛而没有食欲。</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/640-70/#main\" rel=\"attachment wp-att-85362\"><img srcset=\"http://cdn.chokstick.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/640-3.gif 2x\" src=\"http://cdn.chokstick.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/640-3.gif\" alt=\"800\" width=\"800\" height=\"225\" class=\"rank\" style=\" \">11.</strong> 爱讨价还价的人不适合纹身</p>\n<p>纹身，不是化妆，这是一辈子的投资。 </p>\n<p>一个带在身上一辈子的图案，分分钟比你老婆陪你的时间还久。</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/38763a61b3d20db6b3220512ce713ac3/#main\" rel=\"attachment wp-att-85365\"><img </p>\n<p><a href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/640-4-23/#main\" rel=\"attachment wp-att-85366\"><img class=\"aligncenter size-full wp-image-85363\" /></a></p>\n<p>嫌贵的话，还是画一个吧。</p>\n<p>不然去了黑店纹了个歪脸，还要再花几倍价钱覆盖，这就尴尬了。</p>\n<p><a class=\"aligncenter size-full wp-image-85368\" /></a></p>\n<p style=\" margin-top: 40px;color:#000;font-size:1.3em;font-weight:bolder;border-bottom: 1px solid #d9d9d9; padding-bottom: 5px;\"><strong srcset=\"http://cdn.chokstick.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/640-8.jpg 2x\" src=\"http://cdn.chokstick.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/640-8.jpg\" alt=\"640\" width=\"800\" height=\"1033\" href=\"http://chokstick.com/myway/2020/02/a-guide-to-your-first-tattoo/attachment/ecc635d61ea829265931c8b80a088507/#main\" rel=\"attachment wp-att-85372\"><img butterfly\" width=\"1200\" height=\"1200\" class=\"aligncenter size-full wp-image-85373\" /></a></p>\n<p>确认没问题了，就静静地等待纹身师发功，给你一个惊喜吧。</p>\n<p><a
