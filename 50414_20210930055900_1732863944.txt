I also shortened the front and back to make it land at high hip rather than low hip. </p><p class=\"\">I cut the yokes on the grain (the stripes ran horizontally on this fabric), and the sleeves and front/back on the cross-grain.</p><p class=\"\">I dropped the armpits down an inch (you can see this in the photos below).
