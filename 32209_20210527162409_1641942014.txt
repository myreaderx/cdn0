It's refusing to give up.\"</p> \n   <p>\"The greatest illusion is that life should be perfect.\"</p> \n   <p>The artworks are nice. They were loosely drawn with pen, ink and watercolour. The drawings have a children-book style to them. They also look like drafts or rough sketches for more polished illustrations. The style suits the subject matter. And all the text were handwritten. </p> \n   <p>There are 128-pages in this hardcover. You can go through this book under an hour. </p> \n   <p>It's a nice book but I still don't understand why it's so popular.
