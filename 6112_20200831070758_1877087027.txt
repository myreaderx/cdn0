href=\"https://amp.dev/documentation/guides-and-tutorials/learn/amp-caches-and-cors/how_amp_pages_are_cached/#can-i-opt-out-of-caching\">ページが AMP Cache から提供されるのを防ぎます</a>（たとえば、Google 検索や Bing href=\"https://github.com/ampproject/amp-toolbox\">AMP Toolbox</a> の新しい <a href=\"https://github.com/ampproject/amp-toolbox/tree/master/packages/runtime-fetch\">fetch-runtime
