Gather all of the stones in your hand. Then, toss them onto the ground in front of you. Try to keep the stones as close together as possible.<sup id=\"_ref-21\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 21\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-21\">[21]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-21.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 21.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/3/39/Play-Five-Stones-Step-21.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-21.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div></li> \n  <li>Pick up 2 stones in your dominant hand and toss 1 in the air. That way, you can work from right to left or left to right as you pick up each stone.</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Catch the tossed stone in the same hand. After you pick up the new stone, open your palm to catch the stone you threw. Make sure you don’t drop either stone.<sup id=\"_ref-24\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 24\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-24\">[24]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-24.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 24.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/e/eb/Play-Five-Stones-Step-24.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-24.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>You should now have 2 stones in your hand again.</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Repeat the process until you’ve exchanged all of the stones on the floor. Toss 1 stone in the air, then exchange the stone in your hand with 1 on the floor. Pick up a different stone than the last one. Catch the stone that you threw in the same hand, just like before. Continue until you’ve held all of the stones on the floor in your hand.<sup id=\"_ref-25\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 25\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-25\">[25]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-25.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 25.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/2/23/Play-Five-Stones-Step-25.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-25.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>At the end of this round, you’ll be holding 2 stones in your hand.</li>\n   </ul></li>\n </ol> \n <h3><span class=\"mw-editsection\"><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">[</span><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/index.php?title=Play-Five-Stones&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6\" title=\"Edit section: Doing the Juggle Round\">Edit</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">]</span></span><span class=\"mw-headline\" id=\"Doing-the-Juggle-Round\">Doing the Juggle Round</span></h3> \n <ol>\n  <li>Toss the 2 stones in your hand up into the air. Unlike with the other rounds, don’t worry about throwing all of the stones on the ground. Instead, start with the 2 stones you’re already holding in your hand.<sup id=\"_ref-26\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 26\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-26\">[26]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-26.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 26.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/7/74/Play-Five-Stones-Step-26.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-26.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>If you already put the stones down, you might pick them up and begin the round like normal. However, some people say that this ends your turn, as if you dropped them.</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Pick up a stone with the same hand while the stones are in the air. As soon as you release the stones into the air, grab 1 of the stones that are still on the floor. Make sure you use the same hand you used to throw the stones. Then, immediately get into position to catch the 2 stones you threw.<sup id=\"_ref-27\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 27\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-27\">[27]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-27.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 27.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/6/63/Play-Five-Stones-Step-27.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-27.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div></li> \n  <li>Catch the 2 stones you threw in opposite hands. Unlike in previous rounds, you’ll use both hands for this move. As the stones come down, catch 1 stone in the hand that’s holding the stone you picked up and the other in your free hand.<sup id=\"_ref-28\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 28\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-28\">[28]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-28.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 28.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/c/c3/Play-Five-Stones-Step-28.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-28.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>Reach for whatever stone is closest to that hand.</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Throw the 2 stones in the same hand up into the air. Keep the single stone in your non-dominant hand. Use your dominant hand to toss the other 2 stones you’re holding up into the air.<sup id=\"_ref-29\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 29\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-29\">[29]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-29.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 29.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/4/41/Play-Five-Stones-Step-29.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-29.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div></li> \n  <li>Scoop up the 4th stone in the same hand. While the 2 stones are still in the air, grab 1 of the remaining stones off of the floor. Use the same hand that you used to throw the stones.<sup id=\"_ref-30\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 30\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-30\">[30]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-30.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 30.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/a/ad/Play-Five-Stones-Step-30.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-30.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>You should still have 1 stone left on the floor.</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Catch the tossed stones in opposite hands. Like the last time, catch 1 stone in your non-dominant hand and 1 stone in your dominant hand. Be careful that you don’t drop any of the stones in your hand.<sup id=\"_ref-31\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 31\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-31\">[31]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-31.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 31.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/7/71/Play-Five-Stones-Step-31.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-31.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>At this point, you should have 2 stones in each of your hands.</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Repeat 1 more time to gather the 5th stone. Hold onto the 2 stones in your non-dominant hand. Then, toss the 2 stones you have in your dominant hand. Scoop up the 5th stone in your dominant hand, then catch the 2 stones you threw in opposite hands.<sup id=\"_ref-32\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 32\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-32\">[32]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-32.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 32.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/d/d2/Play-Five-Stones-Step-32.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-32.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>You’ll finish this round with 3 stones in your non-dominant hand and 2 stones in your dominant hand.</li>\n   </ul></li>\n </ol> \n <h3><span class=\"mw-editsection\"><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">[</span><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/index.php?title=Play-Five-Stones&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7\" title=\"Edit section: Finishing the Game\">Edit</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">]</span></span><span class=\"mw-headline\" id=\"Finishing-the-Game\">Finishing the Game</span></h3> \n <ol>\n  <li>Toss the 5 stones on the ground and ask your opponent to pick 1. Gather the stones in your hand and throw them one more time. Remember to try to keep them as close together as possible. Let your opponent pick a stone for you to toss in this final round.<sup id=\"_ref-33\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 33\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-33\">[33]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-33.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 33.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/3/39/Play-Five-Stones-Step-33.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-33.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>If you’re playing in a big group, ask the person to the right or left of you to pick.</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Throw the stone and scoop up the other 4. Use your dominant hand to toss the stone up in the air. Before it falls, collect all 4 stones on the floor using the same hand. Be careful that you don’t drop any of the stones.<sup id=\"_ref-34\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 34\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-34\">[34]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-34.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 34.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/1/17/Play-Five-Stones-Step-34.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-34.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>This will be much easier if the stones are close together.</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Catch the falling stone in the same hand. Open your hand and try to catch the stone you tossed before it falls. Make sure that you don’t drop any of the other 4 stones as you do this.<sup id=\"_ref-35\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 35\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-35\">[35]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-35.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 35.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/2/26/Play-Five-Stones-Step-35.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-35.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>If you catch the stone without dropping any, you’ve officially finished all of the moves!</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Award 1 point for each completed round without dropping any stones. If you want to play multiple rounds, give yourself a point each time you make it through all of the steps. Try to see who can earn the most points by completing multiple rounds without dropping any of the stones.<sup id=\"_ref-36\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 36\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-36\">[36]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-36.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 36.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/2/23/Play-Five-Stones-Step-36.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-36.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>Keep track of how many points each person gets so you can check the final score.</li>\n   </ul></li> \n  <li>Give the win to the person who gets the most points. Like with most games, the person who has the highest score wins. When you’re tired of playing or run out of time, check the score to find your winner!<sup id=\"_ref-37\" class=\"reference\" aria-label=\"Link to Reference 37\"><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Play-Five-Stones#_note-37\">[37]</a></sup>\n   <div class=\"mwimg  largeimage  floatcenter \" style=\"max-width:728px\">\n    <a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/Image:Play-Five-Stones-Step-37.jpg\" class=\"image\"><img alt=\"Play Five Stones Step 37.jpg\" src=\"https://www.wikihow.com/images/thumb/e/eb/Play-Five-Stones-Step-37.jpg/aid3231181-v4-728px-Play-Five-Stones-Step-37.jpg\" decoding=\"async\" width=\"728\" height=\"546\" class=\"whcdn\" /></a>\n   </div> \n   <ul>\n    <li>If you want to play a shorter game, give the win to the person who finishes all of the moves first.</li>\n   </ul></li>\n </ol> \n <h2><span class=\"mw-editsection\"><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">[</span><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/index.php?title=Play-Five-Stones&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8\" title=\"Edit section: Things You'll Need\">Edit</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">]</span></span><span id=\"Things-You'll-Need\"></span><span class=\"mw-headline\" id=\"Things-You.27ll-Need\">Things You'll Need</span></h2> \n <ul>\n  <li>5 stones or small objects</li>\n </ul> \n <h2><span class=\"mw-editsection\"><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">[</span><a target=\"_blank\" href=\"https://www.wikihow.com/index.php?title=Play-Five-Stones&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9\" title=\"Edit section: Tips\">Edit</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">]</span></span><span class=\"mw-headline\" id=\"Tips\">Tips</span></h2> \n <ul>\n  <li>When you're good at the game, try adding in a clap or a knee slap before picking up the stones.</li>\n </ul> \n <h2><span
